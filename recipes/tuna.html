Tuna

Description:

Tuna is a delicious meal, it is healthy and full of protein.

Ingredients:

  • Tune.
  • Half an onion.
  • Salt.

Steps:

  1. Add oil to the frying pan and heat it.
  2. Add the onion to the frying pan.
  3. Add the tuna.
  4. Add the salt before the cooking is finished.
  5. Remove the food when the cooking is done.
