Chicken Steak

Description:

This is a very easy to make meal, it is fast to make and delicous.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast.
  • broccoli.
  • Three slices of lemon.
  • Olive oil.
  • Salt.

Steps:

  1. Add olive oil to the frying pan.
  2. Add salt to the breast chicken steaks.
  3. Add the chicken to the frying pan.
  4. Leave frying for 5 minutes on each side.
  5. Add broccoli.
