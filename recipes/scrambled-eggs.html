Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is one of the easiest and fastest foods to cook,it is also healthy.

Ingredients

  • Eggs.
  • Salt.

Steps:

  1. Break the eggs and put them in a glass.
  2. Add salt.
  3. Stir the eggs.
  4. Add oil to the frying pan.
  5. Continously move the eggs in the frying pan.
  6. Stop cooking when the egg is fried.
